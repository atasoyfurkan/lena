OPTIMA BLOOD GLUCOSE METER

Intended Use

The monitoring system is used for diagnostic purposes from outside the body (in vitro). Set consists blood glucose meter and test strips. Fresh capillaries taken from the fingertip to measure blood glucose level should be used with whole blood. It is suitable for home and clinical use.

Features

  • No coding is required.
  • Most accurate results in just 6 seconds.
  • Measuring with a small amount of blood sample.
  • It displays the result by counting down on a large, readable screen.
  • You can monitor 120 test results through the retrospective memory.
  • 2 years warranty.
