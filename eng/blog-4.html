The Importance of ExercIse When You Have DIabetes

In diabetes, nutrition therapy and medication and insulin treatment are generally emphasized, while exercise is neglected. However, exercise is an important part of diabetes treatment as much as nutrition and medical treatment.

Why is exercise important in diabetes?

Exercise, which increases physical activity, decreases blood sugar by speeding up the use of sugar formed by the breakdown of food by muscle tissues. Especially in overweight diabetics, increasing the energy consumed by exercise helps to decrease body weight. Besides, exercise provides physical fitness.

Should I consult my doctor to start exercising?

Do not forget to consult your doctor about the type and duration of exercise you plan to do before starting a program. Every diabetic who will start the exercise program must first get a doctor's check. Otherwise, an unrecognized cardiovascular or kidney disease can cause problems during a continuous and heavy exercise program. If you have hypertension, coronary heart disease, lung and vascular system diseases, it will be useful to be seen by an ophthalmologist and evaluated for retinopathy before planning a long-lasting, tiring exercise program. Exercise is not recommended for diabetics with complications such as retinopathy, nephropathy, diabetic foot.

When and how often should exercise be done?

Many believe that the most beneficial way of exercise is in the morning on an empty stomach. However, exercising on an empty stomach may cause a rise in blood glucose levels. For this reason, people with diabetes should not exercise on an empty stomach. The best time to exercise is 1-2 hours after eating. In this way, the sugar that gets into the blood with food is easily used by the body and the blood sugar glucose level can be controlled. There is a risk of hypoglycemia of exercising three hours before or after meals. The exercise should be planned and at least 3-4 times a week. However, this should not mean exercising 3 consecutive days a week and not exercising other days. The most convenient is to exercise regularly, every day. If you are fat and aim to lose weight, do it every day on a planned basis.

What should be the duration and type of exercise?

Muscles do not use glucose in short (1-5 minutes) exercise programs. Therefore, the exercise program should start with 5 minutes and its duration should be gradually increased. At least 20-30 minutes should be spent on an effective exercise program. This time can be increased over time to 60 minutes. An exercise program should definitely include 5 minutes of warm-up, 5-10 minutes of stretching, 20-30 minutes of physical activity, and 5 minutes of cooling. Instead of area-specific exercise, we should prefer operating the whole body. (Instead of bodybuilding, they can prefer exercises that activate all muscle groups in the body such as walking, running, swimming.)
