About Us

Lena Group was established in 2011. Since its establishment, it has been working on health solutions in cooperation with its national and international partners that aim to protect and improve human health. It carries out its activities responsibly, transparently, and adhering to high ethical standards against its teammates, customers, business partners, and society. Lena Group focuses on competitiveness, employee happiness, and quality and never compromises on customer satisfaction and its values. Group aims to continue by developing human health with diabetes-oriented products as well as innovative products within the growing areas of healthcare services.
